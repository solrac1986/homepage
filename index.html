My interests

I am looking for challenging projects in Privacy, Human Behavior,Computer Vision, UI/UX (HCI) with Augmented Reality (AR) applications.

My main research interest are Internet-of-Things, privacy, mobile augmented reality, human-computer-interaction, social networks, and device-to-device communication.

Furthermore, I am an amateur photographer who loves travelling around the world to meet new landscapes, people and cultures.
